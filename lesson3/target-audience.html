Target Audience and Scenarios

The target audience will be people in the area of Bulverde city looking for local businesses and event and offers they might be offering.

Personas

  1. Families

    1. Around 30 years old
    2. High school with some College
    3. New to the area
    4. Married with kids

  2. Singles

    1. Around 30 years old
    2. Attending College
    3. New to the area
    4. Single

Scenarios

The following scenarios will be used to inform the user of the site:

  • A list of business by topic or area?
  • What events are happening this soon?
  • What events would be good for my kids?
  • Who would be a good roofer to fix my house?
